\begin{enumerate}
    \item Prove that the set of total functions from $\naturals \rightarrow \naturals$ is uncountable by diagonalization.

    \item Let $$A_{CFG} = \{\langle G, w\rangle~|~ G \text{ is a CFG that generates string } w\}.$$
    Prove that $A_{CFG}$ is a decidable language.

    \item Consider a Language $A$ such that $A$ is undecidable but Turing acceptable (such as $A_{TM}$) show that $\overline{A}$ is non-Turing Acceptable.

    \item Prove $A_3 = \{\langle M, w\rangle ~|~|L(M)|=5\}$, the problem of deciding whether a machine accepts exactly 5 strings, is undecidable. (Use reduction from $HALT_{TM}$ to the problem in question.)
\end{enumerate}
